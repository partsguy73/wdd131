Optimization

Optimization

Responsive Design? At a time where mobile devices dominate online traffic, a responsive design is essential. TimcoDesigns ensures that your website looks and functions flawlessly on all devices and screen sizes. This not only provides a better user experience but also boosts your search engine rankings, as Google prioritizes mobile-friendly websites.

Search engine optimization? TimcoDesigns builds all of our designs with Search Engine Optimization as our goal. Search Engine Optimization or SEO for short uses three factors to establish how a website is ranked, clean code, fast load times, and other SEO-friendly architecture. Using techniques learned from many sources, our designers use various establish methods to write code that is easy to follow and thoroughly checked for accuracy. We also use optimization and compression to effectively reduce the size of images to the smallest size while maintaining proper aspect ratios. SEO-friendly architecture includes things like site security, structured data markup, and internal linking to increase your websites ranking. This equals more traffic which equals more users and more overall revenue.
